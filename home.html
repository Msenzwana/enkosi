Enkosi Group

Enkosi is a proudly Black woman-owned business that offers a unique blend of services designed to make everyday living and special occasions seamless and memorable. Founded by a dynamic young entrepreneur, Enkosi stands as a testament to excellence, innovation, and community upliftment.

We specialise in three core areas:
  • Catering – Fresh, flavourful, and beautifully presented meals tailored for any event.
  • Rental Properties – Comfortable, well-maintained spaces for individuals and families seeking a place to call home.
  • Equipment Hire – A wide range of high-quality equipment for events, parties, and corporate functions.

At Enkosi, our passion is service. With a strong commitment to reliability, quality, and empowerment, we aim to not only serve but to inspire and grow—one client, one event, one property at a time.
